📅 Today
  • 🚄Depart Zhangjiajie West ~10:00. Short and easy journey — enjoy the last mountain views.
  • 🏨Arrive Chongqing East ~12:30. Take Metro to Jiefangbei area (解放碑) — stay in this district for best access to everything.
  • 🏮Afternoon: Hongya Cave (洪崖洞) — an 11-story riverside stilted building packed with restaurants and shops. The night view is one of the most photographed scenes in China.
  • 🌆Evening: walk along the Yangtze and Jialing riverbanks for the jaw-dropping night skyline. Chongqing is built on hills — every view is dramatic.
  • 🌶️Dinner: Chongqing hotpot (重庆火锅) — VERY different from Sichuan hotpot. Redder, more numbing, spicier. Order the 鸳鸯锅 (half spicy, half mild) if you're cautious.
