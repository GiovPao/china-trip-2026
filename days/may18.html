📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Stay in Wulingyuan, NOT in Zhangjiajie city (they are 30km apart). Book a hotel close to the north gate of the park.


💡 Tip: May is rainy season in Zhangjiajie — pack a foldable rain jacket. Rain creates sea-of-clouds effects between the pillars that look absolutely magical.


📍 From the reels (key geography tip): Zhangjiajie has two completely separate areas ~1 hour apart by car — Wǔlíngyuán (武陵源) for the forest park and Yǒngdìng (永定) for the city and Tianmen Mountain. You're correctly staying in Wulingyuan. The ENG reel recommends the area near the East Gate of the National Forest Park (东门) as the best base — everything you need is within walking distance. The ESP reel also suggests trying to stay in or near Gǔlín Yuán village (谷林苑村), right at the park gate, to get in before the tour groups.


📍 Park ticket: ¥225 (~€28), valid for 4 consecutive days. The 4-day pass includes all internal shuttle buses. No need to rush — you have time across two full days.


📍 Local food tonight: The ENG reel recommends Sānxiā Guō (三下锅) — a local-style hotpot — and rock ear mushroom chicken soup (石耳炖鸡). Simple, very authentic Zhangjiajie dishes.
