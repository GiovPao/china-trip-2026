📅 Today
  • 🏯Morning: Yu Garden (豫园) and the Old City — a classical Ming Dynasty garden surrounded by a bazaar. Go early before it fills up. The garden itself is genuinely beautiful.
  • 🎨Tianzifang (田子坊) — a maze of narrow alleyways in the French Concession with indie boutiques, cafes, and art studios. Very photogenic.
  • 🏙️Shanghai Tower observation deck (上海中心) — the second tallest building in the world. Views on a clear day are incredible. Book online.
  • 🌆Farewell dinner — treat yourself. The French Concession has some of the best restaurants in China for any cuisine.
