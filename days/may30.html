📅 Today
  • 🏯Morning: Yu Garden (豫园) and the Old City — a classical Ming Dynasty garden surrounded by a bazaar. Go early before it fills up. The garden itself is genuinely beautiful.
  • 🎨Tianzifang (田子坊) — a maze of narrow alleyways in the French Concession with indie boutiques, cafes, and art studios. Very photogenic.
  • 🏙️Shanghai Tower observation deck (上海中心) — the second tallest building in the world. Views on a clear day are incredible. Book online.
  • 🌆Farewell dinner — treat yourself. The French Concession has some of the best restaurants in China for any cuisine.

📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Shanghai Tower observation deck tickets: book online to skip queues (~180 CNY). Best visibility in the morning.


💡 Tip: Tianzifang is best in the afternoon/evening. Yu Garden is best before 09:30. Plan your day around this.


📍 From the reels: For Tianzifang, enter from Lane 210, Taikang Road — not the main Taikang Road entrance which is more tourist-facing. There's a small local market deeper in the alleyways. For the Shanghai Tower, the skip-the-queue ticket is worth the extra ~30 CNY — saves 45 min. The SWFC Sky Walk next door is cheaper and gives a different angle.
