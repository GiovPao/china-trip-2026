📅 Today
  • 🏘️Morning: Ciqikou Ancient Town (磁器口) — a well-preserved Ming/Qing Dynasty town on the Jialing River. Narrow alleyways, tea houses, street food, artisan shops.
  • 🚡Afternoon: Yangtze River cable car (长江索道) — a working commuter cable car crossing the Yangtze. Short but iconic. ~10 CNY, incredible views.
  • 🌄Nanshan Hill (南山) — take a taxi up for panoramic views over the entire city and both rivers. 'One Tree Hill' viewing platform is the classic spot.
  • 🌶️Final dinner in Chongqing — try some local specialities beyond hotpot: 小面 (xiǎo miàn, spicy noodles), 抄手 (wonton soup), 酸辣粉 (sour and spicy glass noodles).
