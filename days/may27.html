Notes & reminders:

Pack tonight — early train to Shanghai tomorrow (~08:30 departure).


📍 Lama Temple (from reels): Far less visited than other Beijing landmarks — more authentic atmosphere. The 18m sandalwood Buddha is extraordinary. The ESP reel confirms it as a hidden gem even many Beijing visitors skip.


📍 Hutong area to explore: The ENG food reel specifically recommends the Shícháhǎi (什刹海) hutong area for wandering — traditional alleyways, preserved architecture, street food stalls. Look for Ròu jiā mó (肉夹馍) — meat in flatbread — from street carts.


📍 Evening bar area: The ESP reel recommends the Hòuhǎi (后海) lake bar strip for live music. The ENG reel warns: check reviews on Amap (高德地图) before sitting down anywhere — some bars seriously overcharge tourists. Stick to places with good ratings.


📍 From the reels: The ENG hutong reel reveals a local dumpling spot hidden in an alleyway near the Drum Tower — ask your rickshaw driver for 鼓楼饺子 (Drum Tower dumplings). The creator of the ITA reel also says the free incense given at Lama Temple gate should be offered at the first burner, not carried around — check the signage.
