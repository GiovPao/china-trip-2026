📅 Today
  • 🌅START AT 07:30 — enter the park at opening to avoid crowds and catch morning mist. Take the shuttle bus to Yuanjiajie (袁家界).
  • 🏔️Yuanjiajie scenic area: the Hallelujah Mountain (inspiration for the floating mountains in Avatar), Enchanting Terrace overlook, Viewing platform walkways.
  • 🛗Bailong Elevator (百龙电梯) — the world's tallest outdoor lift, 326m, built into the cliff face. Incredible experience. Take it DOWN to save your knees.
  • 🌲Afternoon: Tianzi Mountain (天子山) — even more dramatic pillar formations, often wrapped in cloud. Take the cable car up.
  • 🦶Return to hotel by shuttle bus. Feet will be tired — hot shower and a good dinner in town.

📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Best overlooks: Enchanting Terrace (迷魂台), Tianxia Diyi Qiao (Heaven-Linking Bridge). Check weather the night before — cloudy days can be the most dramatic.


💡 Tip: Wear good walking shoes — the paths are uneven stone steps. Bring snacks and water. There are some food stalls inside the park but limited and pricey.
