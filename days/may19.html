📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Park ticket: ~¥225 (~€28), valid for 4 consecutive days — you don't need to rush everything into one day.


📍 Key entry tip (from ESP reel): Enter through the Gǔlín Yuán gate (谷林苑门) rather than the main entrance — it puts you much closer to the Avatar mountain area and saves a lot of internal shuttle bus time. This is the single most impactful planning tip for the park.


📍 Suggested route today (from ENG reel): Take the Bàilóng Elevator (百龙电梯) → explore Yuánjiājie (袁家界) and Yángjiājie (杨家界) → descend through Tiānzǐ Mountain (天子山).


💡 Atmosphere tip: The ESP creator says misty mornings make the limestone pillars look like they genuinely float. Don't be disappointed by cloud — it's often more magical than a clear day.


💡 Practical: Wear good walking shoes — uneven stone steps throughout. Bring snacks and water; park stalls are limited and pricey.


📍 From the reels: The ENG Bailong Elevator reel confirms: go DOWN the elevator, not up — you'll save your legs and the view of the cliff face as you descend is more dramatic. Queue time is 15–30 min in the morning; up to 1h in the afternoon. Buy tickets at the base.
