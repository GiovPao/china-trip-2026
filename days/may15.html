📅 Today
  • 🛕Morning: Wenshu Monastery (next to hotel) — monks chanting, incense, beautiful Tang-dynasty architecture. Free.
  • 🏮Jinli Ancient Street (锦里古街) — restored Han Dynasty-style street with crafts, snacks, and teahouses. A bit touristy but fun.
  • 🏘️Kuanzhai Alley (宽窄巷子) — Wide and Narrow Alley, a beautifully restored historic neighbourhood with courtyards and cafes.
  • 🍵Afternoon: sit in a teahouse in People's Park (人民公园) — locals play mahjong, get their ears cleaned, watch the world go by. Pure Chengdu life.
  • 🌶️Dinner: authentic Sichuan hotpot — Haidilao (海底捞) is reliable and fun, or ask your hotel for a local recommendation.
