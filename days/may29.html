📅 Today
  • 🌿Option A — Suzhou (苏州): 45 min by HSR from Hongqiao. UNESCO-listed classical Chinese gardens, silk industry, canals. Best gardens: Humble Administrator's Garden (拙政园) and Lingering Garden (留园).
  • 🚤Option B — Zhujiajiao Water Town (朱家角): 1h by bus or taxi from central Shanghai. An ancient water town with canals, arched bridges, and traditional houses. Much closer, no train needed.
  • 🛍️Both options are half-day trips — you can be back in Shanghai by 15:00 for more exploring.
  • 🍣Evening: explore the Former French Concession further, or try a rooftop bar with Bund/Pudong views.
