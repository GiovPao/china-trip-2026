📋 Book in advance

Pre-book Terracotta Warriors tickets — they sell out in peak season. Opens 7 days ahead. Go after noon (tour groups arrive in the morning).

📅 Today
  • 🌅Morning free — sleep in or visit the Big Wild Goose Pagoda early (quieter in the morning).
  • 🚌Head to the Terracotta Warriors AFTER 12:00. Take Bus 306 or tourist bus from Xi'an Railway Station (~1h, 7 CNY). Or book a half-day private tour from your hotel.
  • 🏺Terracotta Warriors Museum — Pit 1 (the main hall, massive), Pit 2 (partially excavated, very interesting), Pit 3 (command HQ). Allow 3–4 hours minimum.
  • 🚲Return to Xi'an by 17:00. Evening: rent a bike on the City Wall (城墙) and cycle the full 14km loop — incredible views of the old city.
  • 🍖Dinner: Defachang Dumpling Restaurant near the Bell Tower, or more Muslim Quarter street food.
  • 🌙The Xi'an reel reveals a hidden rooftop bar inside the City Wall with sunset views — accessible from the South Gate area. Perfect after the bike ride around the wall.
