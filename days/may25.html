📋 Book in advance

Book Great Wall tickets + cable car + toboggan in advance at mutianyugreatwall.com or Trip.com. No gate sales in 2025.

📅 Today
  • 🚌Depart hotel ~07:30 to Mutianyu Great Wall. Options: Bus 916 from Dongzhimen + local bus (cheapest, 2h), or booked minibus from hotel (easiest, ~150 CNY roundtrip).
  • 🏯Mutianyu (慕田峪) — the most beautiful restored section, far less crowded than Badaling, no fake shops. Tower 14 to Tower 23 is the classic walk (1.5h, manageable).
  • 🚠Take the cable car UP (saves energy), and the toboggan DOWN (a metal slide down the mountain — genuinely fun, adults love it).
  • 🌿On the wall: walk between watchtowers, look north into the mountains and south into the valley. On a clear day you can see dozens of towers snaking over the ridgeline.
  • 🦆Return to Beijing by 16:00. This is a good night for Peking Duck dinner if you've booked — you've earned it.
