📅 Today
  • 🚡Tianmen Mountain (天门山) is a SEPARATE area from the national forest park — different ticket, different bus. Take the city bus from Wulingyuan to Zhangjiajie city centre (30 min).
  • 🚠Tianmen Mountain Cable Car — the world's longest passenger cable car at 7.5km. Takes 30 min and passes directly over the city. Absolutely spectacular.
  • 🪟Tianmen Cave (Heaven's Gate, 天门洞) — a natural arch at 1,264m. 999 steps up to the arch — challenging but totally worth it.
  • 🪟Coiling Mountain Road glass walkway — if you're brave, the glass-floored walkway on the cliff edge. Hold on tight.
  • 🌆Return to Wulingyuan by 17:00. Pack your bags tonight — early train to Chongqing tomorrow.

📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Tianmen Mountain ticket ~258 CNY (includes cable car). Buy online in advance to skip queues. The glass walkway is an additional small fee.


💡 Tip: Do Tianmen Mountain on a separate day from the national park — they are very different experiences and both are full days. You've planned this correctly.


📍 From the reels: The creator recommends doing Tianmen on a weekday if possible — weekend queues for the cable car can be 45 min+. The glass walkway costs extra (~68 CNY) and is worth it. Do the 999 steps going up to Heaven's Gate — the descent by road gives better views of the mountains below.
