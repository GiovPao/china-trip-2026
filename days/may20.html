📝 My notes

Notes & reminders:

Tianmen Mountain is a separate ticket, separate area from the forest park — a full dedicated day. Budget ~¥258 for the combined ticket.


📍 Correct order — from the ENG reel: Most people waste their visit by doing it in the wrong sequence. The right order is:

1. Take the city cable car (天门山索道) up — 7,455m, 28 minutes, views of the famous 99 bends (99道弯) the whole way. The cable car ride itself is a highlight.

2. At the summit, do the West route first: Glass Walkway (玻璃栈道) → Guǐgǔ Plank Road (鬼谷栈道) on the cliff edge → Tianmen Mountain Temple (天门山寺, originally Tang Dynasty).

3. Take the small internal cable car across to the East route: mountain-penetrating escalator → aerial walkway → base of Tianmen Cave (天门洞).

4. Climb the 999 steps up to Tianmen Cave Square — the natural arch is massive and extraordinary.

5. Descend via Route B (bus down the 99 bends road) — a completely different and equally dramatic experience.


💡 Bonus: Tianmen Cave is illuminated at night and even more spectacular. The sunrise from the arch is also extraordinary if you're up early enough.


📍 From the reels: The creator recommends doing Tianmen on a weekday if possible — weekend queues for the cable car can be 45 min+. The glass walkway costs extra (~68 CNY) and is worth it. Do the 999 steps going up to Heaven's Gate — the descent by road gives better views of the mountains below.
