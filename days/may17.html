📅 Today
  • 🐼Giant Panda Breeding Research Base — ARRIVE AT 08:00. Pandas are most active in the morning (feeding time). By 10:30 they're usually napping. Take Metro Line 3 to Panda Ave station.
  • 🐼In the base: see giant pandas (adults), red pandas, and if lucky, baby pandas in the nursery. Allow 2–3 hours.
  • 🏞️Optional afternoon: Leshan Giant Buddha (乐山大佛) — 71m tall Tang Dynasty Buddha carved into a cliff. 2.5h by bus or 1.5h by fast bus from Xinnanmen station. Full afternoon trip.
  • 🍜Or: slower afternoon in Chengdu — more teahouses, massage, explore Taikoo Li shopping area (beautiful open-air mall next to a 1,600-year-old monastery).

Notes & reminders:

Decide between Leshan day trip or relaxed Chengdu afternoon. Leshan is magnificent but a long day — up to you depending on energy after the wedding.


💡 Tip: At the Panda Base, go to the red panda area first (less crowded) then work your way to the giant pandas. Bring a light jacket — it's air-conditioned in the buildings.


📍 From the reels: The creator confirms: pandas are fed and active from 08:00–10:00, then they eat bamboo for 30 min and sleep the rest of the day. Arrive at the gate at 07:45. The red pandas (小熊猫) are equally adorable and much less crowded than the giant panda enclosures.
